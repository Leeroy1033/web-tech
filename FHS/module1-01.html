Module 1: An Introduction to Food Health and Safety

Module 1: An Introduction to Food Health and Safety provides a foundation for understanding the importance of food safety and hygiene in the food industry. This module covers the basics of food-borne illness, the causes and consequences of food contamination, and the measures that can be taken to prevent it.

This module covers:

  • Topic 1: Food-borne illnesses
  • Topic 2: Causes of Food Contamination
  • Topic 3: Personal Hygiene Practices
  • Topic 4: Occupational Hygiene Practices
  • Topic 5: Temperature Control
